Reporting bugs

Help us help you.

A good bug report includes your plugdata version, your operating system and version, the host (if any), a clear description of what you expected vs. what happened, and ideally a minimal patch that reproduces the issue. Crash logs are gold. Bug reports on Discord are good, but sometimes they can get lost over time: to ensure your bug report is not forgotten, it's best to report it on GitHub.
